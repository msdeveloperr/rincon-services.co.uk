Container Haulage From the Port of Felixstowe

The Port of Felixstowe is the largest container port in the United Kingdom, handling approximately 4 million TEUs annually. As one of the busiest ports in Europe, it requires a network of reliable haulage companies to move containers efficiently from the port to destinations across the UK.

Rincon Services Ltd provides professional container haulage from Felixstowe to anywhere in England, Scotland, and Wales. Our dedicated fleet of over 20 HGV vehicles ensures your containers are collected promptly and delivered safely to their final destination.

Our Felixstowe Container Haulage Services

We handle both standard and specialist container movements from the Port of Felixstowe, including:

  • 20ft standard containers — single and multi-drop deliveries across the UK
  • 40ft containers (standard and high cube) — direct port-to-door transport
  • Import container collection — timely pickup from Felixstowe with onward delivery
  • Export container delivery — loaded container transport to port for shipping
  • Empty container repositioning — returning empty containers to the port or depot
  • Overweight and out-of-gauge loads — specialist movements with appropriate permits

Why Felixstowe Container Haulage Requires Experienced Carriers

Operating at the Port of Felixstowe demands more than just an HGV and a driver. Carriers need to understand port procedures, booking systems, Vehicle Booking Systems (VBS), demurrage timelines, and container terminal operations. Delays at port cost money — both in detention charges and downstream supply chain disruption.

Rincon Services has years of experience working with Felixstowe's terminals including Hutchison Ports' Trinity Terminal and Landguard Terminal. Our drivers are familiar with port protocols, reducing turnaround times and avoiding costly delays.
